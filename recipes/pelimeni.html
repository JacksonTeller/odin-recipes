Pelimeni

Description

Traditional Russian dish that reminds raviolis or dumplings in some way.

Very easy to cook and in the same time very nutritious, pelimeni is very popular

amongst students.

Ingredients:

  • a pack of pelimenis
  • salt
  • mayonnaise or sour cream

Steps:

  1. Fill a pot with 0.4 litres of water and put on high-heat.

    Add a tablespoon of salt and wait untill it starts boiling.

  2. Put the pelimenis in the water and wait for 2 minutes.

    Stir the pelimenis and make sure that they didn't stick

    to the bottom of the pot.

  3. Wait untill the pelimenis start to float.

    It will be the sign that they are cooked.

  4. Eat pelimenis in a bowl with bross, add some mayannaise

    or sour cream to make them better.
