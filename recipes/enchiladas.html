Enchiladas

Description

Beat taco night with enchilada night!

These restaurant-style spicy enchiladas will surely please a crowd.

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 pound lean ground sirloin or turkey
  • 1 1/2 cups diced red or green bell pepper
  • 1 package taco seasoning
  • 8 flour or corn tortillas
  • 2 cups Traditional Cut Shredded 4 Cheese Mexican
  • 1 1/2 cups salsa or picante sauce
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup chopped tomato
  • 1/2 cup light sour cream (Optional)
